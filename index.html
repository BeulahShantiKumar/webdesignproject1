Welcome

Patrick W. McGonagle & Co, is a Second Generation Solicitors Practice based on North Street, Swords, Co. Dublin.

Our firm has been servicing the local community, businesses in Swords and surrounding areas for 27 years on a broad range of legal matters.

Patrick W. McGonagle & Co, guarantee to make your legal transaction run as smoothly as possible. We ensure that our clients are kept fully informed of all developments in their transaction. We believe that communication is the key to a successful and long lasting solicitor and client relationship.

The Solicitors and Support Staff of Patrick W. McGonagle & Co, aim to provide a personal service to the highest standards to our clients, whilst providing a cost efficient service in a confidential matter.
